Welcome to Downtown Clawson

Independent shops, James Beard-nominated restaurants, a magic shop, a distillery, and a weekend farmers market — all on a few walkable blocks of Main Street and 14 Mile Road.

This is Clawson's Little City, Big Heart district. Come grab a coffee, browse the shops, and see what's new.
