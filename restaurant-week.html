Clawson Restaurant Week

March 22–28, 2026. Celebrating the diverse culinary traditions that make Clawson's dining scene unique as part of the national USA250 anniversary.

Event Overview

March 22–28, 2026. This year's Restaurant Week ties into the USA250 celebration of America's 250th anniversary, honoring the immigrant culinary traditions that shape Clawson's food scene.

Participating restaurants will offer featured specials priced between $15 and $25. Pick up a Restaurant Week Passport, collect stamps at different locations, and enter to win prizes.

The event is open to all Clawson restaurants. Follow our social channels for the full list of participants and menus as the date approaches.
