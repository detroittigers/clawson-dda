Saturday Clawson Market

The Saturday Clawson Market is held on listed dates in Downtown Clawson at the intersection of 14 Mile Road and Main Street.

2026 Market Dates & Times

Regular market hours are 10 AM - 2 PM.

  • Saturday, May 16, 2026: 10 AM - 2 PM
  • Saturday, June 13, 2026: 10 AM - 2 PM
  • Saturday, June 27, 2026: 10 AM - 2 PM
  • Saturday, July 11, 2026: 10 AM - 2 PM
  • Saturday, July 25, 2026: 3 PM - 8 PM
  • Saturday, August 15, 2026: 10 AM - 2 PM

Saturday, July 25, 2026 runs in tandem with the Cinema In The Streets event, which starts at dusk on Main Street.
