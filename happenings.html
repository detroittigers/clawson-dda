Annual Events and Programs

Cinema In The Streets

Jul 25, 2026

A section of downtown is closed to traffic for an outdoor movie screening under the stars. Bring chairs and blankets for a family-friendly evening on Main Street.

Down On Main Street Car Show

Aug 8, 2026

Classic cars, hot rods, and custom vehicles line Main Street for a summer car show. Browse the vehicles, grab food from downtown restaurants, and enjoy live music.

Clawson Fest

Aug 22, 2026

Main Street block party celebrating local tunes, tastes, and artistry. Live music all day, food vendors, a dedicated Kids' Zone with face painting and games, and local artist exhibits.

Trick or Treasure

Late October

Kids in costume trick-or-treat at participating downtown businesses for a safe, walkable Halloween experience. Check participating storefronts for treats and activities.

The Great Pubkin Crawl

Late October

A Halloween-themed pub crawl through downtown bars and restaurants. Dress up, grab seasonal drink specials, and make the rounds along the Social District.

Small Business Saturday

Late November

The Saturday after Thanksgiving, downtown shops offer special promotions and holiday shopping deals. A great time to support local and start holiday gift-buying.
